You Trust Us With Your Data,
Learn More About How We Protect It

Revenulytics commitment to trust begins and ends with privacy, security and compliance. We strive to not only comply and implement best practices, but to continuously excel and adapt as technology continues to evolve.

Privacy

As an industry leader, our goal is to make our practices as transparent as possible and to give you control over your data. Privacy is at the core of what we do, which is why we are at the forefront of driving and adopting industry standards and best practices.

Security

Security is embedded throughout our organization, from our solutions to the people. We've put the controls and processes in place to safeguard your data, taking a risk-based approach and making continuous improvement a mandate.

Compliance

Revenulytics uses highly resilient and secure cloud-based solutions to service its providers. The security of the infrastructure and data is a foundational requirement. This must be demonstrated consistently both to maintain client trust and for regulatory and compliance reasons.

Reliability

Deployed in the cloud or on-premises, our infrastructure is designed to deliver stable solutions so our providers can scale with confidence. Revenulytics SOC 1 report provides assurance that our team has designed an effective system of security, availability and confidentiality controls.

Ethics

Ethics and values form the foundation of what we do and how we do it. At Revenulytics, we are one team grounded in integrity, trust, and accountability. Our actions are guided by and taken in accordance with our Code of Ethics and the principles within that Code.

ESG and Sustainability

We are built on trust, and our commitment to protecting human rights, caring for communities, and strengthening our society is at the heart of our work. We nurture responsible business in three ways: lead by example; share our knowledge with the world; and offer solutions that helps our providers accelerate their ESG integration. Code.

Certifications

SOC 1 Type II Report Security Controls

Revenulytics annually executes and completes a SOC 1 for Service Organizations: Trust Services Criteria (i.e., a SOC 1 Type II Report on Controls at a Service Organization Relevant to Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality or Privacy). Revenulytics is happy to provide a copy of the SOC 1 Type II Report upon request under NDA.

PCI DSS

Revenulytics has been certified as compliant with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) v3.2.1.
